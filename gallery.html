BALLROOM FOYER LOUNGE

The Ballroom Foyer Lounge allows your guests to mingle and get ready for the dance floor or conference in the adjacent Ballroom. here they can have their passes and coats checked and get beverages and event programs.

THE BALLROOM MAIN STAGE

The Ballroom’s main stage is equipped with everything you need for a professional production and our AV production staff can help you get oriented or entirely manage your event’s stage production tasks.

THE STREET LEVEL LOUNGE

Our lower floor (443 Broadway), has 2 fully licensed lounges with dance floors and performance spaces with over 6,000 sq. ft. of interior space. This lounge is fully equipped with a high quality sound system, built-in stages and a VIP area.
